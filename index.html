About the Institute

Serving Communities Since 2009

Prakriti Jeev Raksha Sansthan (PJRS) is a registered non-governmental organization committed to the holistic development of society through education, environmental protection, and socio-economic empowerment. We work at the grassroots level, bringing transformative programs to underserved communities across Rajasthan.

Our institute bridges the gap between formal education and practical skill-building, with a special focus on rural youth, women, and environmentally vulnerable communities. Guided by the values of integrity, inclusivity, and service, PJRS has touched thousands of lives and continues to grow its impact year after year.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities with knowledge, skills, and environmental awareness for sustainable development.

Our Vision

A self-reliant, educated, and environmentally conscious India where every citizen has equal opportunity to grow.

Core Values

Integrity, compassion, inclusivity, and sustainable action guide every program we deliver.

Recognition

Registered under the Societies Registration Act with affiliations to State Government bodies.
